Couple Quick Tips to Before you start

1: Presets are pre-loaded. But, you are able to save what you made by overwriting desired presets
When "Slider" is in your desired postion, press "Save" It will default to its original postion. Then press "Apply"
You will now see your saved "Preset['number']"

2: Check volume (If place in middle it's normal)

3: The Octave has 3 ranges. Far Left: (One Oct. above middle C.) Far Right: (One Oct. Below Middle C.)

4: There are 4 types of Waveforms. In this order: (Sine, Square, Sawtooth, Triangle)

5: HB says to have fun and Enjoy Honey Bear's Synthesizer!!!
